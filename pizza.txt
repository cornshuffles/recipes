Ingredients:
Dough:
4 cups bread flower
3 teaspoons sugar
3/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
4 teaspoons olive oil
3/2 cup water
1 packet yeast
Toppings:
pizza sauce
mozarella cheese
toppings

Prep:
Mix water, yeast, and sugar first. Mix in all other ingredients
Let dough sit for ~1 hour to rise. Make pizza.

Bake 450 F for ~15 mins
