Ingredients:

Cake:
1 cup all-purpose flower
3/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
5 large eggs (seperated)
1 cup granulated sugar
1/3 cup whole milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Milk Mixture:
12 ounces evaporated milk
14 ounces sweetened condensed milk
1/4 cup whole milk

Whipped Topping:
1 pint heavy whipping cream
3 tablespoons powdered sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
ground cinammon
sliced strawberries

Prep:
Step 1:
Preaheat oven to 350Â° F. Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking pan

Step 2:
In a medium bowl, combine flower, baking powder, and salt. Seperate eggs into two other mixing bowls.

Step 3:
Add 3/4 cup sugar into egg yolks. Mix until smooth and pale yellow. Add 1/3 cup whole milk and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract into yolk mixture. Mix until combined.

Step 4:
Add yolk mixture into flower mixture. Mix GENTLY until combined. Do not overmix.

Step 5:
Whip egg whites until stiff peaks form. Gradually mix in 1/4 cup sugar. Fold the egg whites into the batter mixture GENTLY. Do not overmix.

Step 6:
Pour batter into pan. Bake for 25-35 minutes.

Step 7:
Remove from oven and allow to cool completely.

Step 8:
Combine 12 ounces evaporated milk, 14 ounces sweetened condensed milk, and 1/4 cup whole milk into a bowl. Mix.

Step 9:
Poke small holes in the top of the cake with a toothpick. Evenly pour the milk mixture over the top of the cake.

Step 10:
Refrigerate for at least one hour, preferrably overnight.

Step 11:
Mix 1 pint heavy cream, 1/2 teaspoon vanilla, 3 tablespoons powdered sugar. Whip until stiff peaks.

Step 12:
Top the cake with the whip. Sprinkle ground cinammon on top of the whip. Serve with sliced strawberries.
