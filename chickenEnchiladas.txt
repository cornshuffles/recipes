Ingredients:

Enchiladas:
2 tablespoon olive oil
1 small white onion, peeled and diced
3/2 pounds chicken breasts, diced into small 1/2-inch pieces
1 (4-ounce) can diced green chiles
salt and pepper
1 (15-ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
8 large flour tortillas
3 cups Mexican-blend shredded cheese

Toppings:
cilantro
diced red onions
avacado
sour cream and/or crumbled cojita cheese

Sauce:
2 tablespoon olive oil
2 tablespoons flour
1/4 cup chili powder
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
2 cups chicken stock
salt

Prep:

Sauce:
Heat oil in saucepan, add flour, cook for 1 minute whilst whisking. Add chili powder, garlic powder, cumin, and oregano, cook for 1 minute whilst whisking.
Gradually pour in stock whilst whisking.
Simmer until thickened ~10-15 mins.
Salt to taste.

Enchilada:
Preheat to 350 F. Prepare sauce.
In a pan, saute the onion. Add diced chicken and green chiles. Season with salt and pepper. Cook chicken. Add in beans, stir until homogenous. (remove the bean juice first!)
Assemble the enchiladas, sauce, filling, cheese.
Bake uncovered for ~20 mins.
Add toppings.
